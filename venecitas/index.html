Venecitas

One day I was browsing Twitter very unmerrily (as one does) when I came across this tweet. It caught my attention how it described something so ordinary to me as peculiar to a certain particular artistic movement. So I looked at the picture and two things happened. My first thought was how much beauty there must be around us which is lost on us as it becomes no more than background noise in our day to day lives.

My second thought was "I could probably CSS that".

This was my first experiment with 3D CSS transformations, so I'll probably come to revisit this code later and tidy it up here and there. The feel of the wall behind isn't quite right, and the stairs aren't in the right height, but I'm very proud of that handle. I'm afraid there's also a little javascript here doing some heavy lifting, as I couldn't figure out how to generate a bazillion tiles with a random distribution of colours.
